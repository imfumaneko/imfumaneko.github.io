Our Mission

We are a purpose-driven organisation that provides afterschool intervention program to primary schools, we also provide facilitator program to students, those students are predominantly matric learners not sure what to do after matric, some are improving some subjects to apply to varsity the next year and some are studying.

Our Vision

Our aim is to contribute to an increased number of enhanced learner performance in English, Xhosa, and Mathematics, we believe our facilitators are a much-needed support structure to the schools.

Our Approach

We are bringing in not so formal form of education yet effective as we play a big brother or sister role to the learner, bridging the gap between teacher and learner, which makes learners more comfortable to engage.

Services

Academic Intervention Program

We are in partnership with Samora Machel Primary School offering academic intervention for Mathematics, Xhosa, and English for Grade 4 to Garde 7.

Connect Academic Intervention Facilitation

We have a work readiness program called Connect. We have partnered with young students from Samora Machel township to offer facilitation to Samora Machel Primary School.

Terra Litta Consulting

Assist with all needed trainings for the Academic Intervention and Connect program.
